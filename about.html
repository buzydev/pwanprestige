About Us

PWAN PRESTIGE REALTIES & INVESTMENT LIMITED is an affilate of pwan group real estate that was launched Officially on the 8th of december,2022. PWAN is an acronym of Property World Africa network. Pwan Group is a wholly Nigerian company registered with C.A.C to carry out real estate trading, sales and acquisition.

We have been in business since 2012 during which we have birthed over 40 affilates thereby growing into a group of comapanies. Pwan group was founded by Dr Augustine Ozioma and Dr Jayne Obioma Onwumere "The real estate power couple,"We offer real estate investment products for wealth creation thereby helping our clients achieve their financial dreams.
